Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prospect Connect?
Prospect Connect is a job finder platform, the platform is primarily used for professional networking and career development, and allows jobseekers to post their CVs and employers to post jobs. personal networking site to assist and organized employment opportunities for an individual.
Does Prospect Connect offers hybrid and fulltime jobs?
Yes, we do. It depends on the suggested jobs feed showed on your homepage.
How do I know if I get a job offer on the website?
Callbacks are showed in the network page, there will be job offers provided on your page when companies reply back to you.
